Seven-Step Method

Our onboarding method is designed with collaboration in mind. To ensure a seamless and productive partnership, we work hand in paw throughout the whole process.

From understanding your business goals to executing effective marketing strategies, we’re here to support and act as your guide every step of the way.

1. Discovery

Learn what your business is all about and understand your objectives. We’ll have a thorough discussion to get a grasp of your marketing needs.

2. Definition

Together we’ll set clear expectations when we define the scope of work, deliverables, timelines, and communication channels to ensure we’re aligned.

3. Research

Evaluate your current marketing efforts to identify initiatives we can strengthen and gaps that we can fill. Conduct research to understand your target audience.

4. Ideation & Development

Craft a marketing strategy that outlines each tactic. Develop and create the assets that will bring your story to life.

5. Recommendation

Present our recommendations to explain how it aligns with your objectives. Feedback is crucial as we finalize the plan together.

6. Execution & Optimization

After approval, we launch campaigns with technical precision. Monitor performance to make adjustments for optimization.

7. Review & Refinement

Keep you informed on progress with check-ins. Evaluate the effectiveness of our efforts and make necessary refinements.
